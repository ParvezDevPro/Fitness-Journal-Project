Endurance Training:

Cardiovascular Endurance:

• Definition:Endurance exercises, such as running or cycling, improve cardiovascular health.

• Benefits:Strengthened heart, improved circulation, and reduced risk of heart disease.

Weight Management:

• Definition:Endurance activities burn calories and contribute to weight control.

• Benefits:Weight management, improved fitness levels, and enhanced overall well-being.


Disease Prevention:

• Definition: Regular endurance exercise is associated with a lower risk of certain cancers.

• Benefits:Reduced risk of breast and colon cancer, and prevention of chronic conditions like hypertension.
