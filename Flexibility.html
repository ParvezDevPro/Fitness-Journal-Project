Flexibility Training:

Joint Mobility:

• Definition:Flexibility exercises, such as stretching, improve joint mobility.

• Benefits:Enhanced range of motion, reduced stiffness, and improved overall joint health.

Mind-Body Connection:

• Definition:Flexibility exercises often involve mindfulness and body awareness.

• Benefits: Improved mental focus, stress reduction, and a positive impact on mood.


Injury Prevention:

• Definition: Flexibility training helps prevent injuries by promoting balanced muscle development.

• Benefits:Reduced risk of injury, improved posture, and overall musculoskeletal health.
