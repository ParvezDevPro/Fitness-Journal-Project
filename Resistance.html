Resistance Training:

Muscle Strength and Definition:

• Definition:Resistance training, such as weightlifting, helps build and define muscles.

• Benefits:Improved muscle tone, increased metabolism, and enhanced overall strength.

Bone Density:

• Definition:Weight-bearing exercises contribute to bone density.

• Benefits:Reduced risk of osteoporosis and improved skeletal health.


Metabolic Health:

• Definition: Resistance exercises can positively impact insulin sensitivity and metabolic health.

• Benefits:Management of blood sugar levels, reducing the risk of type 2 diabetes.

    BodyWeight Exercises:
  • Push-ups
  • Pull-ups
  • Squats
  • Lunges
  • Planks
    Weightlifting Exercises:
  • Bench Press
  • Deadlifts
  • Bicep Curls
  • Tricep Dips
  • Leg Press
